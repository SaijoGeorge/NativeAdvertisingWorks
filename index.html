Native Advertising Examples - Case Studies

Native advertising is paid content that matches a publication’s editorial standards while meeting the audience’s expectations. While it may sound like it's no different than an advertorial, there are some key differences. A good native ad should align with the publication or site’s established editorial style and tone and should provide the same sort of content the publication’s audience typically expects. Adverisers and brands love native advertising because there is higer engagement.
